Acknowledgements



     Emory's Electronic Poetry Project is under the direction of project advisors: Dr. Geraldine Higgins (Emory University), Dr. Edna Longley (Queen's University, Belfast), and Dr. Ronald Schuchard (Emory University). Content of the pages is the responsibility of Dr. Stephen Enniss, Curator of Literary Collections at Emory University's Robert W. Woodruff Library. The electronic texts reproduced here have been prepared by Dr. Charles Spornick of the Woodruff Library's Beck Center for Electronic Collections and Services and his staff. Web page design is by Jason Lemon.

     Stephen Enniss and the project advisors wish to thank Michael Allen, Philip Hobsbaum, and Michael and Edna Longley for their willingness to answer many questions over the course of this project. Dr. Marilynn Richtarik has also shared information about the Belfast Group from her forthcoming biography of Stewart Parker. Michael Smallman, from the Queen's University Library, has also been quite helpful in providing access to the Group sheets held in the Irish Collection in the Queen's Library. Finally, we also wish to thank Philip Hobsbaum, Michael Longley, Paul Muldoon, and James Simmons for permission to include their own Group sheets in this electronic project.
